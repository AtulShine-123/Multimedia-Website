LOG_01 WHY THESE PIECES? +
I selected these three specific works because they represent the widest range of techniques I learned this semester. They demonstrate my competency in layer masking, vector pen tool precision, and color grading theory.
LOG_02 CREATIVE PROCESS? +
My favorite aspect was the experimental phase of the 'Neural Network' piece. Discovering how blending modes (specifically Difference and Exclusion) interact created unexpected visual textures that I couldn't have planned manually.
LOG_03 FUTURE IMPROVEMENTS? +
If I were to revisit these, I would focus heavily on typography integration. I feel the visual elements are strong, but the text hierarchy within the posters could be more legible and stylistically aligned with the graphic elements.
