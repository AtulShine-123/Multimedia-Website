LOG_01 WHY THESE PIECES? +
Black and White Synthwave: I chose this piece because I really wanted to experiment with negative space and high contrast. The Synthwave aesthetic is usually very colorful, so stripping it down to just black and white forced me to rely entirely on line and shape to make the image recognizable. It represents my interest in retro-futurism but with a minimalist twist.

The Owl: This vector illustration was chosen because it shows my progress with creating organic forms using digital tools. I wanted to focus on clean lines and distinct blocks of color rather than blending. It stands out in my portfolio because it feels like a professional logo or sticker design, which is a style I enjoy creating.

The Porsche GT3 RS: I selected this car illustration because it was my most ambitious project regarding detail and color. I love automotive design, and I wanted to capture the aggressive shape of the GT3 RS. It contrasts well with my other two pieces because it uses a vibrant, multi-colored background and complex mechanical lines compared to the simpler nature scenes.
LOG_02 CREATIVE PROCESS? +
Black and White Synthwave: My favorite part of creating this was working on the perspective grid. Getting the lines to converge towards the "sun" created a strong sense of depth and movement. It was satisfying to see a flat image suddenly look like a 3D landscape just by manipulating the lines.

The Owl: The best part of this process was designing the shadow and highlight shapes on the feathers. Instead of using a gradient tool, I had to manually draw the jagged shapes to imply texture. It was fun figuring out exactly where the light source (the moon) was hitting the bird and how that changed the value of the brown feathers.

The Porsche GT3 RS: I really enjoyed coloring this piece, specifically the background stripes. Matching the rainbow background to the race car livery was fun because it made the whole image pop. I also liked drawing the wheels; even though they were difficult, getting the geometric pattern of the spokes right felt like solving a puzzle.
LOG_03 FUTURE IMPROVEMENTS? +
Black and White Synthwave: If I were to improve this, I would add more texture to the palm trees. Right now, they are solid white silhouettes, but adding some small black cuts or lines inside the leaves would give them more definition and make them look less flat against the grid.

The Owl: I would change the background to add more atmosphere. While the blue box works as a frame, adding a subtle gradient or maybe some clouds behind the moon would create more emphasis on the owl. I also think the claws on the branch could be defined better to make the bird look like it's gripping the wood tighter.

The Porsche GT3 RS: I would work on the value and shading on the car's body. The colors are accurate, but the car looks a little flat. Adding darker shadows underneath the spoiler and lighter highlights on the hood would give the car more form and make it look 3D, rather than just a sticker on a background. I‚Äôd also clean up the "GT3 RS" text in the back to make the lines smoother.
